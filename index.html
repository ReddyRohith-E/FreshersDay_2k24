About SWAGATH 2024

SWAGATH 2024 - Ignite the Spark

Welcome to SWAGATH 2024 at MITS College, Madanapalle! This event is crafted to ignite excitement and foster camaraderie among our newest academic members. We're here to celebrate the arrival of our SWAGATH, ensuring they get a memorable introduction to the vibrant life awaiting them on campus.
SWAGATH 2024 is not just an event; it's a tradition marking the start of an incredible journey at MITS. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment where new students can make friends and build lasting connections. With a variety of activities catering to diverse interests, there's something for everyone.
The event begins with a grand opening ceremony, featuring inspiring speeches from esteemed faculty members and senior students. This is followed by ice-breaking sessions designed to help our SWAGATH get to know each other in a fun, relaxed setting. These activities not only entertain but also ease the transition into university life at MITS College.
Ready to ignite your spark?
